Sushi

An easy Sushi recipe that can be prepare in home easily.

Sushi rolls can be filled with any ingredients you choose.Try smoked salmon instead of imitation crabmeat. Serve with teriyaki sauce and wasabi.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice (cooked rice seasoned with vinegar, sugar, and salt)
  • Raw fish (such as tuna, salmon, or yellowtail)
  • Seafood (like shrimp, crab, or squid)
  • Vegetables (commonly cucumber, avocado, or pickled daikon)
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi (Japanese horseradish)
  • Pickled ginger (gari)

Steps

  1. Cook sushi rice according to package instructions, then let it cool slightly.
  2. Prepare sushi vinegar by mixing rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, then fold it into the cooked rice.
  3. Cut nori (seaweed sheets) to desired sizes for sushi rolls.
  4. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat, spread a thin layer of rice evenly over the nori, leaving about 1 inch at the top uncovered.
  5. Add your choice of fillings (such as fish, seafood, vegetables) along the bottom edge of the rice.
  6. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat, sealing the edge with a dab of water.
  7. Repeat steps 3-6 for additional rolls, then slice each roll into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife dipped in water.
  8. Serve sushi with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger on the side.
