Lasagna

An easy lasagna recipe that save a lots of time.

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce or pasta sauce
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Onion, chopped
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Dried oregano and basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef (or sausage) until browned; drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet with the cooked meat; simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, shredded mozzarella, and grated Parmesan.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles over the sauce, followed by half of the ricotta mixture, and more sauce.
  8. Repeat with another layer of noodles, ricotta mixture, sauce, and top with shredded mozzarella.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
